# MeetBarter SARL - 03: Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

**BETWEEN**:
**MeetBarter SARL** (The "Company"), a Lebanese limited liability company.
AND
**[Investor Name]** (The "Investor").

## 1. THE INVESTMENT

The Investor agrees to pay the Company the sum of **$[Amount]** (the "Purchase Amount") in exchange for the right to certain shares in the Company's capital, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

## 2. TRIGGER EVENTS (Equity Conversion)

This SAFE will convert into preferred shares of the Company upon the occurrence of:

* **Equity Financing**: A transaction or series of transactions resulting in capital injection exceeding a pre-defined threshold.
* **Liquidity Event**: A change of control, merger, or initial public offering.
* **Dissolution**: A voluntary or involuntary cessation of operations.

## 3. VALUATION CAP & DISCOUNT

* **Valuation Cap**: $[Amount]
* **Discount Rate**: [Percentage]%

## 4. REGULATORY ALIGNMENT (The "Anti-Token" Provision)

The Investor acknowledges that the Company operates as a Technological Middleware Platform. This SAFE grants rights to **EQUITY** in the SARL entity. It does **NOT** grant rights to "Value Points," "Tokens," or any "Digital Assets" that might be classified as securities or currency under Lebanese Law 133 or BDL Circulars.

## 5. REPURCHASE RIGHT

The Company reserves the right (but not the obligation) to repurchase this SAFE or the resulting equity at Fair Market Value in the event of a regulatory change that requires the Company to remain a closely-held entity.
