# MeetBarter SARL - Simple Agreement for Future Tokens (SAFT)

## BETWEEN

MeetBarter SARL (The "Company"), a Lebanese limited liability company.
AND
**[Investor Name]** (The "Investor").

## 1. THE PARTICIPATION

The Investor agrees to pay the Company the sum of **$[Amount]** (the "Investment Amount") in exchange for the right to acquire a future allocation of Platform Exchange Units ("Value Points" or "VP") or any successor digital asset, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

## 2. TRIGGER EVENT (Token Generation Event - TGE)

Upon the formalization of the MeetBarter Protocol and the mass distribution of protocol-based accounting units (the "TGE"), the Company shall issue to the Investor a number of Units equal to the Investment Amount divided by the **Discounted Unit Price**.

## 3. DISCOUNT & VALUATION

* **Discount Rate**: [Percentage]%
* **Valuation Cap**: $[Amount] (Applied to the protocol's theoretical network value).

## 4. REGULATORY ALIGNMENT (The "Anti-Financial" Shield)

The Investor acknowledges that the Units are **Functional Utilitary Assets** designed for use within the MeetBarter technological ecosystem.

* They are NOT securities.
* They are NOT currency.
* The Investor participates in the **Utility Growth** of the protocol, not a financial speculative instrument.

## 5. DISSOLUTION & CONVERSION

If a TGE does not occur within [Number] months, the Company may, at its discretion:

1. Convert this SAFT into equity (SAFE) in MeetBarter SARL at a pre-defined valuation.
2. Refund the Investment Amount (subject to available liquidation capital).

## 6. JURISDICTION

This agreement is governed by the laws of Lebanon, with any disputes settled in the Commercial Courts of Beirut.
