# MeetBarter SARL - Registration Execution Manual

This manual provides the tactical steps required to execute the legal registration of MeetBarter SARL in Lebanon, based on the **Legal Registration Doctrine v1.0**.

## PHASE 1: PREPARATION & DRAFTING (Current Status)

* [x] **Draft Articles of Association**: Completed (template at `/institutional/legal/registration/articles_of_association.md`).
* [x] **Regulatory Shield Strategy**: Completed (defined in `/institutional/legal/registration/regulatory_shield_doctrine.md`).
* [ ] **Select Lawyer**: Appoint a licensed Lebanese attorney (required for SARL formation).
* [ ] **Registered Address**: Confirm lease or title deed for the company's fiscal domicile.

## PHASE 2: CAPITALIZATION

1. Take the drafted Articles of Association to a chosen bank in Lebanon.
2. Request the opening of a **"Company Under Formation"** account.
3. Deposit the statutory capital of **5,000,000 LBP**.
4. **Deliverable**: Obtain the official Bank Deposit Certificate.

## PHASE 3: NOTARIZATION

1. Meet with the Notary Public (Kاتب عدل).
2. Present the Articles of Association and Manager's identity documents.
3. Notarize the **Articles of Association** and the **Manager Appointment**.
4. **Deliverable**: Authenticated Incorporation Deed.

## PHASE 4: THE COMMERCIAL REGISTER (Sijil el Tejari)

1. Your lawyer must submit the dossier to the Commercial Register at the relevant District Court.
2. The dossier includes the Notarized Articles, Bank Certificate, and Lease Contract.
3. Pay the required stamps and fees.
4. **Deliverable**: The official **Commercial Register Extract** (Afedeh Ikariya) and Registration Number.

## PHASE 5: FISCAL REGISTRATION (Ministry of Finance)

1. The Manager or Lawyer must visit the Ministry of Finance (MoF).
2. Register the company for Income Tax.
3. Obtain the **Tax Identification Number (TIN)**.
4. **Deliverable**: Official MoF Registration Certificate.

## SUCCESS CRITERIA CHECKLIST

* [ ] Official Stamp of the Commercial Register.
* [ ] Active Tax ID.
* [ ] Bank account unfrozen (converted to Corporate Account).
* [ ] Trademark "MeetBarter" filed (Parallel process).
