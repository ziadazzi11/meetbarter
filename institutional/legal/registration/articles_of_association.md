# MeetBarter SARL - Articles of Association (Draft Template)

**Entity Name**: MeetBarter SARL (Limited Liability Company)
**Headquarters**: Zouk Mosbeh, Lebanon
**Capital**: 5,000,000 LBP (Five Million Lebanese Pounds)

---

## ARTICLE 3: OBJECT OF THE COMPANY

The Company is established with the primary objective of:

1. **Development and Operation of Digital Information Platforms**: Engineering, deploying, and maintaining software environments that facilitate the organized communication and exchange of information between private individuals and legal entities.
2. **Technological Middleware Services**: Providing data management and algorithmic verification systems to enhance the transparency of peer-to-peer bartering and asset exchanges.
3. **Marketplace Management Systems**: The licensing and management of digital interfaces for the listing, categorization, and search optimization of goods and services.
4. **Data Analytics and Tech-Consulting**: Providing technological insights and data systems for optimization of supply chains and local exchange circles.
5. **International Licensing**: The right to license its proprietary software architecture and brands to foreign entities.

**EXCLUSION CLAUSE**: The Company categorically excludes from its object any activity reserved for financial institutions, banking entities, currency exchange houses, or regulated payment processors as defined by Lebanese Law (Code of Commerce, Code of Money and Credit). The Company does not accept deposits, does not issue money, and does not act as a custodian of monetary funds.

---

## ARTICLE 4: INTERNAL EXCHANGE UNITS (VALUE POINTS)

The Company provides a standardized accounting mechanism within its platform known as "Value Points." These units are strictly **Non-Monetary Platform Facilitation Tools** designed to measure relative bartering weight. Holders of Value Points have No Claim of redemption or conversion into Lebanese Pounds or any foreign currency against the Company.

---

## ARTICLE 8: GOVERNANCE & LIABILITY

The Company is managed by a Single Manager (the "Manager") who has the broadest powers to act in the name of the Company within the limits of its Object Clause. The Manager's liability is strictly limited to the capital of the company, save for cases of gross negligence or criminal intent as defined by Lebanese Law.

---

## ARTICLE 21: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any dispute arising from the interpretation of these articles or the operation of the platform shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the **Beirut Commercial Court**, applying Lebanese Law.
