# NGO Board Roles & Institutional Responsibilities

**Entity**: MeetBarter Community Association

## 1. THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS (BOD)

The BOD is responsible for the strategic direction of the non-profit arm and ensuring its operations remain strictly non-commercial.

### President

* Official representative of the Association before the Ministry of Interior and other legal bodies.
* Signs off on all official "Ilm wa Khabar" (Notification) documents.
* Chief spokesperson for the community mission.

### Secretary General

* Manages the minutes of meetings and official correspondence.
* Coordinates with the General Assembly.
* Ensures compliance with the 1909 Association Law.

### Treasurer (CFO)

* Manages the Association's bank account (Separate from SARL).
* Responsible for financial transparency and reporting to donors.
* Oversees the allocation of grants for community projects.

## 2. ADVISORY ROLES

* **Community Shield Officer**: Ensures that the "Internal Point" doctrine is followed in the NGO's social swaps to avoid regulatory friction.
* **Tech-Liaison**: Coordinates with MeetBarter SARL for software updates and server access for the Association's modules.
