---

### ARTICLE 1: NAME & GOAL

The Association is named **"MeetBarter Community Association"**. Its objective is to:

1. **Community Empowerment**: Facilitate non-commercial exchange and mutual aid between citizens in Lebanon.
2. **Crisis Resilience**: Develop technological and social systems that allow vulnerable populations to access essential goods and services through bartering.
3. **Digital Literacy**: Provide training and awareness on the use of digital exchange protocols for local economic resilience.
4. **Humanitarian Logistics**: Support the distribution of aid and the optimization of local supply chains through the MeetBarter software.

The Association is a non-political, non-sectarian, and non-profit entity.

---

### ARTICLE 2: MEMBERSHIP

Membership is open to any individual or legal entity committed to the goal of community bartering.

* **Active Members**: Contributing to the daily operations.
* **Honorary Members**: Selected for their strategic support to the mission.

---

### ARTICLE 3: GOVERNANCE

The Association is governed by:

1. **The General Assembly**: Comprising all active members. It is the supreme authority.
2. **The Board of Directors**: Elected from the General Assembly to manage the association's affairs.

---

### ARTICLE 4: RESOURCES & FINANCES

The resources of the Association consist of:

1. Membership dues.
2. Unconditional grants and donations from local and international bodies.
3. Institutional support from MeetBarter SARL in the form of software licensing and infrastructure.

The Association's funds must be used exclusively to fulfill the objectives stated in Article 1.

---

### ARTICLE 5: RELATIONSHIP WITH MEETBARTER SARL

The Association serves as the "Social Heart" of the ecosystem. It manages the humanitarian and community-centric modules of the software (e.g., skill sharing for the elderly, student barter cycles). Any "Value Points" managed by the Association are strictly for non-profit social utility.
