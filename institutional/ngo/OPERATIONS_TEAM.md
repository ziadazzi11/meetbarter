## Team Structure

### 1. **Executive Director / General Manager**

> [!NOTE]
> **Phase 0 (Current):** The Executive Director (Founder) is the *only* staff member.
>
> - **Grant Processing:** Automated by Database Logic.
> - **Community Outreach:** Handled by Founder & Volunteer Ambassadors.
> - **Operations:** Automated via Admin Dashboard.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Lead NGO operations and staff management
- Implement Board decisions and strategic plans
- Oversee all Foundation programs and initiatives
- Primary liaison between Board, LLC, and community

**Key Duties:**

- Daily operational management
- Staff hiring, training, and performance reviews
- Program development and execution
- Budget management and financial oversight
- Stakeholder communication and reporting
- Grant application and fundraising coordination
- Annual report preparation for Board and Ministry

**Required Skills:**

- Nonprofit management experience
- Leadership and team building
- Financial management
- Strategic planning
- Excellent communication

**Reporting:** Reports to Board of Directors

### 3. **Grants & Compliance Officer**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Manage Community Grant Program
- Ensure NGO regulatory compliance
- Process grant applications and disbursements
- Maintain compliance documentation

**Key Duties:**

- Review and approve community grant requests
- Verify grant recipient eligibility
- Disburse VP grants according to rules
- Track grant outcomes and impact
- File annual NGO reports with Ministry of Interior
- Maintain tax-exempt status documentation
- Coordinate external audits

**Required Skills:**

- Grant management experience
- Lebanese NGO law knowledge
- Attention to detail
- Financial literacy
- Compliance and audit experience

**Reporting:** Reports to Executive Director and Board Treasurer
