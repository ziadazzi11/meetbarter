## Subject: Establishment of the "Meetbarter Lebanon" Association

**To the Ministry of Interior and Municipalities,**

In accordance with the Lebanese Law of Associations (August 3, 1909), we, the undersigned founders, hereby submit this notice regarding the establishment of the following association:

### 1. Name of the Association

**Meetbarter Lebanon** (Scientific and Social Association).

### 2. Headquarters

Zouk Mosbeh, Street 73, St. Antoine Building, 5th Floor.

### 3. Purpose & Objectives

The Association aims to modernize local barter systems through secure technology, ensuring the protection of community resources during economic fluctuations. Its primary focus is the management of a "Humanitarian Escrow" designed to allocate 4% of system transaction value to the **Social Shield** emergency fund, providing direct support to Lebanese families in need of food, medicine, and essentials.

### 4. Founders & Board of Trustees

The Association is governed by a technical and administrative board committed to the principles of transparency and forensic auditability.
