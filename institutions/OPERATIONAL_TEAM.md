## Overview

The Operational Support Team handles day-to-day platform operations, user support, moderation, and logistics coordination.

---

## Team Structure

### 1. **Operations Manager**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Oversee daily platform operations
- Coordinate between technical and community teams
- Ensure smooth user experience
- Monitor operational metrics and KPIs

**Key Duties:**

- Daily operations dashboard monitoring
- Incident response coordination
- Process optimization and documentation
- Team coordination and scheduling
- Operational budget management
- Vendor and partner management
- Monthly operations reports

**Required Skills:**

- Operations management
- Process improvement
- Data analysis
- Team coordination
- Problem-solving

**Reporting:** Reports to Executive Director (NGO) and CTO (LLC)

---

### 2. **Customer Support Lead**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Manage customer support team
- Ensure timely and quality user support
- Develop support processes and knowledge base
- Monitor user satisfaction

**Key Duties:**

- Support ticket queue management
- Team training and quality assurance
- Knowledge base article creation
- User satisfaction surveys
- Escalation handling
- Support metrics reporting
- Tool and system management (helpdesk software)

**Required Skills:**

- Customer service management
- Conflict resolution
- Training and coaching
- Technical troubleshooting
- Empathy and patience

**Reporting:** Reports to Operations Manager

---

### 3. **Customer Support Specialists (3-5 team members)**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Respond to user inquiries and issues
- Troubleshoot technical problems
- Guide users through platform features
- Escalate complex issues

**Key Duties:**

- Answer support tickets (email, chat, phone)
- Provide step-by-step guidance
- Document common issues and solutions
- Collect user feedback
- Test new features from user perspective
- Maintain positive user relationships

**Required Skills:**

- Customer service
- Technical aptitude
- Communication skills
- Patience and empathy
- Problem-solving

**Reporting:** Reports to Customer Support Lead

---

### 4. **Content Moderator / Trust & Safety Specialist**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Review flagged listings and content
- Enforce community guidelines
- Identify and address policy violations
- Protect platform integrity

**Key Duties:**

- Review moderation queue daily
- Approve or reject flagged content
- Issue warnings and suspensions
- Document policy violations
- Report patterns to security team
- Update moderation guidelines
- Coordinate with legal on serious violations

**Required Skills:**

- Judgment and discretion
- Understanding of community standards
- Attention to detail
- Emotional resilience
- Cultural sensitivity

**Reporting:** Reports to Operations Manager and Legal Advisor

---

### 5. **Logistics Coordinator**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Facilitate trade logistics and delivery
- Coordinate with delivery partners
- Resolve logistics issues
- Optimize delivery processes

**Key Duties:**

- Coordinate trade pickups and deliveries
- Manage delivery partner relationships
- Track delivery performance
- Resolve delivery disputes
- Optimize delivery routes and costs
- Seasonal logistics planning
- Emergency logistics support

**Required Skills:**

- Logistics and supply chain knowledge
- Coordination and scheduling
- Problem-solving
- Negotiation
- Geographic knowledge of service areas

**Reporting:** Reports to Operations Manager

---

### 6. **Data Analyst / Business Intelligence Specialist**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Analyze platform data and metrics
- Generate insights and reports
- Monitor key performance indicators
- Support data-driven decision making

**Key Duties:**

- Daily/weekly/monthly reporting dashboards
- User behavior analysis
- Trade pattern analysis
- Anomaly detection support
- A/B test analysis
- Predictive modeling
- Data visualization

**Required Skills:**

- Data analysis and statistics
- SQL and database queries
- Data visualization tools
- Business intelligence
- Report writing

**Reporting:** Reports to Operations Manager and CTO

---

### 7. **Verification Specialist**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Verify business and institutional licenses
- Review verification evidence
- Conduct background checks
- Maintain verification standards

**Key Duties:**

- Review business verification requests
- Verify submitted documentation
- Conduct reference checks
- Approve or reject verification applications
- Maintain verification database
- Update verification criteria
- Fraud detection and prevention

**Required Skills:**

- Investigation and research
- Attention to detail
- Judgment and discretion
- Knowledge of Lebanese business regulations
- Document verification

**Reporting:** Reports to Grants & Compliance Officer (NGO)

---

### 8. **Community Moderator / Ambassador Liaison**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Support Ambassador program
- Moderate community forums and discussions
- Facilitate community engagement
- Recognize and reward community contributors

**Key Duties:**

- Ambassador onboarding and training
- Community forum moderation
- Ambassador performance tracking
- Reward distribution (VP bonuses)
- Community event coordination
- Feedback collection from ambassadors
- Ambassador recognition programs

**Required Skills:**

- Community management
- Facilitation and mediation
- Communication skills
- Empathy and patience
- Event coordination

**Reporting:** Reports to Community Outreach Coordinator (NGO)

---

## Team Collaboration

**Daily Huddle:** 15-minute operational sync

**Weekly Ops Review:** Review metrics, issues, and improvements

**Monthly All-Hands:** Cross-team updates and alignment

**On-Call Rotation:** Support and moderation coverage 7 days/week

---

## Tools & Systems

- **Helpdesk:** Zendesk, Freshdesk, or similar
- **Moderation:** Custom admin panel
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, custom dashboards
- **Communication:** Slack, Discord, or similar
- **Documentation:** Notion, Confluence, or wiki

---

## Performance Metrics

- **Support:** Response time, resolution time, satisfaction score
- **Moderation:** Queue clearance rate, accuracy, appeal rate
- **Logistics:** On-time delivery rate, cost per delivery
- **Verification:** Processing time, approval rate, fraud detection

---

## Compensation

**Salaries:** Competitive rates based on role and location

**Benefits:** Health insurance, paid time off, remote work options

**Bonuses:** Performance-based incentives tied to metrics
