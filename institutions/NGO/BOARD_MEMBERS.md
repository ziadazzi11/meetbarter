## Overview

The Board of Directors provides strategic oversight, fiduciary responsibility, and governance for the MeetBarter Foundation NGO.

---

## Board Members & Roles

### 1. **Chairperson / President**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Preside over all Board meetings and General Assemblies
- Set strategic vision and long-term roadmap
- Represent the Foundation in official capacities
- Ensure compliance with Lebanese NGO law and bylaws
- Approve major partnerships and institutional agreements

**Key Duties:**

- Quarterly Board meetings facilitation
- Annual report presentation to Ministry of Interior
- Crisis Protocol activation authority (Protocol 0)
- Final approval on budget allocations >$10,000
- Signatory authority on legal documents

**Required Skills:**

- Leadership and strategic planning
- Understanding of Lebanese NGO regulations
- Experience in nonprofit governance
- Strong communication and diplomacy

---

### 2. **Vice President / Deputy Chair**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Act as Chairperson in their absence
- Oversee day-to-day operational coordination
- Liaison between Board and Executive Team
- Monitor compliance with Foundation bylaws

**Key Duties:**

- Backup signatory for urgent decisions
- Coordinate Board committee activities
- Review monthly operational reports
- Succession planning for key roles
- Disaster recovery protocol oversight

**Required Skills:**

- Operational management experience
- Conflict resolution
- Financial literacy
- Regulatory compliance knowledge

---

### 3. **Treasurer / Financial Officer**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Oversee all financial operations and reporting
- Ensure fiscal responsibility and transparency
- Manage Foundation bank accounts and assets
- Prepare annual financial statements

**Key Duties:**

- Monthly financial reporting to Board
- Budget preparation and monitoring
- Tax compliance and filing
- Audit coordination with external auditors
- Grant fund management and disbursement tracking

**Required Skills:**

- Accounting and financial management
- Audit and compliance experience
- Budget planning and forecasting
- Familiarity with NGO financial regulations

---

### 4. **Secretary / Records Officer**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Maintain official records and meeting minutes
- Ensure proper documentation of all Board decisions
- Manage correspondence and official communications
- Custodian of Foundation bylaws and amendments

**Key Duties:**

- Record and distribute Board meeting minutes
- Maintain member registry and attendance records
- File annual reports with Ministry of Interior
- Archive official documents and resolutions
- Coordinate Board elections and voting processes

**Required Skills:**

- Excellent written communication
- Organizational and archival skills
- Attention to detail
- Knowledge of parliamentary procedure

---

### 5. **Legal Advisor / Compliance Officer**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Provide legal counsel to the Board
- Ensure compliance with Lebanese laws and regulations
- Review contracts and agreements
- Manage legal risks and disputes

**Key Duties:**

- Legal review of all major decisions
- Compliance audits (quarterly)
- Dispute resolution and arbitration
- Intellectual property protection
- Regulatory filings and permits

**Required Skills:**

- Lebanese law degree and bar admission
- NGO and corporate law expertise
- Contract negotiation
- Risk management

---

### 6. **Community Representative**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Represent the interests of platform users and community
- Ensure Foundation serves its mission
- Gather feedback from grassroots stakeholders
- Advocate for user-centric policies

**Key Duties:**

- Quarterly community town halls
- User feedback analysis and reporting
- Ethical oversight of platform policies
- Ambassador program liaison
- Community grant proposal review

**Required Skills:**

- Community organizing experience
- Empathy and active listening
- Public speaking
- Understanding of barter economics

---

### 7. **Technical Oversight Director**

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Oversee technical infrastructure and security
- Ensure platform reliability and data protection
- Review technical roadmap and priorities
- Cybersecurity governance

**Key Duties:**

- Quarterly technical audit reviews
- Disaster recovery and backup verification
- Master Key rotation oversight (6-year protocol)
- Protocol 0 technical implementation review
- Open-source compliance monitoring

**Required Skills:**

- Technical background (software/systems)
- Cybersecurity knowledge
- Risk assessment
- Understanding of distributed systems

---

## Board Meetings

**Frequency:** Quarterly (minimum) + Emergency sessions as needed

**Quorum:** 4 out of 7 members (majority)

**Voting:** Simple majority for most decisions; 2/3 majority for bylaw amendments, Master Key rotations, and Protocol 0 activation

**Minutes:** Recorded by Secretary and distributed within 7 days

---

## Compensation

Board members serve on a **voluntary basis** with no salary. Reasonable expenses for official duties may be reimbursed upon approval.

---

## Term Limits

**Standard Term:** 3 years, renewable once (maximum 6 consecutive years)

**Staggered Elections:** To ensure continuity, elections are staggered so not all positions expire simultaneously

---

## Conflict of Interest Policy

Board members must:

- Disclose any financial interests in platform decisions
- Recuse themselves from votes where conflicts exist
- Prioritize Foundation mission over personal gain
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
