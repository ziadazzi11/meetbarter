## Overview

Ambassadors are trusted community members who verify businesses, onboard new users, and strengthen the MeetBarter ecosystem.

---

## Ambassador Levels

### Level 1: Community Ambassador

> [!TIP]
> **Phase 0 Strategy:** This program is the *primary* operational workforce for the launch.
> Ambassadors are volunteers paid in **Status and VP**, not cash.

**Requirements:**

- 20+ completed trades
- 85%+ trust score
- No policy violations in past 6 months
- Application and background check

**Privileges:**

- Verify Level 1 businesses (basic verification)
- Earn 50 VP per user signup referral
- Earn 100 VP per business verification
- Access to Ambassador Portal
- Exclusive Ambassador badge

**Responsibilities:**

- Verify business documentation authenticity
- Onboard new users in local community
- Provide feedback on platform improvements
- Maintain high ethical standards
- Report suspicious activity

---

### Level 2: Regional Ambassador

**Requirements:**

- Level 1 Ambassador for 6+ months
- 50+ successful verifications
- 90%+ trust score
- Regional coverage commitment

**Privileges:**

- Verify Level 2 businesses (institutional verification)
- Earn 150 VP per Level 2 verification
- Priority support access
- Voting rights on community proposals
- Regional Ambassador badge

**Responsibilities:**

- Cover specific geographic region
- Train and mentor Level 1 Ambassadors
- Organize local community events
- Quarterly regional reports
- Crisis response coordination in region

---

### Level 3: National Ambassador

**Requirements:**

- Level 2 Ambassador for 1+ year
- 200+ successful verifications
- 95%+ trust score
- Board nomination and approval

**Privileges:**

- Verify Level 3 businesses (institutional licenses)
- Earn 300 VP per Level 3 verification
- Seat on Ambassador Council
- Direct line to Board
- National Ambassador badge
- Annual stipend

**Responsibilities:**

- National program oversight
- Policy and guideline development
- Ambassador training program management
- Represent Ambassadors to Board
- International expansion support

---

## Ambassador Duties

### Verification Responsibilities

- Review business registration documents
- Verify physical business location
- Conduct reference checks
- Interview business owners
- Submit detailed verification reports
- Follow up on verified businesses

### Community Building

- Host local meetups and workshops
- Onboard new users with training
- Answer community questions
- Promote platform adoption
- Build trust in local ecosystem

### Quality Assurance

- Maintain verification accuracy >95%
- Report fraudulent applications
- Update verification criteria
- Participate in calibration sessions

## Code of Conduct

Ambassadors must:

- **Integrity:** Verify honestly without bias or favoritism
- **Confidentiality:** Protect sensitive business information
- **Professionalism:** Represent MeetBarter with dignity
- **Transparency:** Disclose conflicts of interest
- **Accountability:** Accept responsibility for verifications
- **Community First:** Prioritize community benefit over personal gain

**Violations may result in:**

- Warning and retraining
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from program
- VP reward clawback

## Application Process

1. **Eligibility Check:** Meet minimum requirements
2. **Application:** Submit online application form
3. **Background Check:** Verification of identity and history
4. **Interview:** Video call with Community Outreach Coordinator
5. **Training:** Complete Ambassador training program
6. **Probation:** 3-month probationary period with supervision
7. **Certification:** Full Ambassador status upon successful completion
